Our Story

Established in 2020 by Phemelo Nyambiwa, Ranewa Healthy Juices began as a humble juice stand near Gaborone’s Main Mall. Driven by a passion for health and sustainability, Phemelo envisioned a brand that would make nutritious, organic beverages and snacks accessible to all. Today, Ranewa is a beloved name in Botswana, sourcing 100% organic ingredients from local farmers to create smoothies and fruit salads that nourish both body and soul. Our commitment to quality and community has fueled our growth, inspiring healthier lifestyles across the region.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Partnering with local farmers, we use organic ingredients to minimize our environmental footprint and support Botswana’s agricultural heritage.

Health First

Our products are free from artificial additives, delivering pure, nutrient-rich goodness to fuel your wellness journey.

Community

We champion local initiatives, from health workshops to youth programs, to foster a stronger, healthier Botswana.

Meet Our Founder
