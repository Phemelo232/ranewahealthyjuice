Tropical Smoothie Bliss

Recipe April 20, 2025

Escape to a tropical paradise with this vibrant smoothie, packed with the sunny flavors of mango, pineapple, and coconut water. Perfect for a quick breakfast or a refreshing snack, this recipe is both delicious and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mango, diced
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass and enjoy!

Tip: Add a handful of ice for an extra refreshing twist.
